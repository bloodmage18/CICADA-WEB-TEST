CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

Apparel Graphic Designer

Apparel graphic designers create individual, original images for clothing. They develop a strong understanding of the brand's customers and ensure their graphics are cohesive and visually appealing to that audience.

Art Director

Art directors are responsible for the visual style of the animation. Art directors start by examining the script and working with the director to understand the vision for the film or TV programme. They then create their designs and determine the tone,mood and colour palettes.

UX/UI Designer

As a user experience/user interface designer, you’ll scenario-based digital and physical user interaction design. Craft every touchpoint in a digital or physical product to ensure brand consistency and emotional appeal.
